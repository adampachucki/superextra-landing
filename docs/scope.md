# Scope

## Feature Categories

### Market Landscape

- Restaurant openings & closings
- Cuisine type distribution by area
- Emerging cuisine types
- Cuisine type trends (growing/declining)
- Competitor ranking & benchmarking
- Top-performing venues & best-sellers

### Menu & Pricing

- Trending menu items
- Menu price tracking
- Competitor price monitoring
- Delivery platform price markups vs dine-in menus
- Deals & promotional activity analysis

### Revenue & Sales

- Estimated revenue
- Sales seasonality patterns
- Revenue split by channel (dine-in, delivery, takeaway)
- Seat occupancy rates
- Platform market share (Pyszne, Wolt, Glovo, Uber Eats etc.)

### Guest Intelligence

- Review analysis & sentiment tracking
- Local guest expectations & preferences (review-derived)
- Tourist vs local visitor mix (review-derived)
- Payment method preferences

### Competitor Marketing & Operations

- Social media & Instagram activity analysis
- Marketing channels & estimated ad spend
- Tools & platforms in use

### Location & Foot Traffic

- Measured foot traffic at specific points & areas
- Estimated foot traffic projections
- Demographic profiles & purchasing power estimates
- Tourist vs local traffic patterns
- Visit time distribution

### Operations

- Local labor pool availability
- Salary benchmarks
- Competing job offer activity
- Staff tenure, turnover & retention rates
- Commercial rent prices & trends
- Supplier pricing trends

> **How it's delivered:** Data sources aggregated into a single platform. AI agents for interactive analysis and monitoring. Human experts delivering tailored research reports and briefings.

# Scope

## Raw Feature Ideas

### Market & Competition
- New openings and closings
- Cuisine types in area
- New cuisine types opening
- Cuisine types trending up/down
- Best performers and best-sellers
- Competitor ranking vs you

### Menu & Pricing
- Menu trending items
- Menu prices tracker
- Competitor price monitoring
- Deals, promotional analysis
- Delivery platforms markups (how prices differ vs normal menu)

### Revenue & Sales
- Estimated sales
- Sales seasonality
- Share of dine-in, delivery, takeaway etc
- Share of seats sold
- Payment methods
- Delivery platforms share of market

### Guest Intelligence
- Analyzing reviews
- Expectations and sentiment from local guests — based on reviews
- Tourists traffic vs local, also from reviews

### Marketing & Digital
- Insights from SM/IG of competitors
- Competitors marketing channels and spend
- Tools and platform used

### Location & Foot Traffic
- Foot traffic in points and areas
- Estimated foot traffic
- Estimated demographics, purchasing power
- Time of visits

### Supply Chain
- Suppliers pricing

### Workforce
- Available staff
- Expected salaries
- Competition in job offers
- Expected tenure, rotation, retention

### Real Estate
- Rent prices and trends

### Research
- Access to collection of reports
